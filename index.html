FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before you get started.

How quickly can I set up my page?

Most businesses are live in under five minutes. Add your services, hours, and a few photos, then share your link.

Do I need a website already?

No. Your SolidW page works as a standalone link you can share on social media, receipts, or a QR code.

Can customers book without creating an account?

Yes. Customers submit a reservation directly from your page, and it lands in your dashboard instantly.

What happens if I outgrow the Free plan?

Upgrade to Pro at any time for unlimited pages, unlimited gallery photos, and higher booking limits.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No contracts. The Free plan is free forever, and Pro pricing is arranged directly with our team.
